Instructions

You can convert your Scratch projects to Flash on my website!
http://junebeetle.github.io/converter/

This allows you to make them into standalone applications, post them on your website, send them to people who don't have Scratch, and share them without sharing the source code.

Notes and Credits

I check these comments occasionally, but if you post a question and I or another Scratcher hasn't answered in a few days, please try contacting me via my website.

All the images and text are done in Adobe Illustrator. The Scratch cat is from Scratch, of course. The Flash symbol is traced from a SWF file icon from Adobe Flash player. The font used is Helvetica.
